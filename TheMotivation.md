# The Motivation

It has been long due. I have been trying to learn technical analysis and apply the patterns in my daily life as a stock trader. Recently, I have moved to trading in futures and after loosing amount, I have realised that it depends on 2 things

1. The News
2. Technical Analysis to some extent.

While I can't control the news, I feel, I can understand the technical analysis part of the stocks and see if I can avoid some obvious pitfalls.

By the end of this exercise, I hope to learn the following.

1. Ability to set up an initial screener to identify stocks of interest.
2. Ability to look at the current chart of a stock and predict with certain level of confidence its tragectory.
3. Ability to predict with a certain level of confidence if a stock is a buy or sell.
4. Ability to predict with a certain level of confidence, the buy or a sell price.
